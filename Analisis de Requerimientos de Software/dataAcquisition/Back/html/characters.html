Luhzio

Our main character is Luhzio, the only non hybrid animal in the island. It is an aventurous purple tiger with pink strips, with a big heart. Luhzio always wanted to be like the other Bam Banimals, but now its flaw is the only thing that can save the other Bam Banimals.

Potatzio

Potatzio is Luhzio's best friend, together they live near the shore. Potatzio is a combination of rabbit and fox, making it easy for it to jump into high places, like the Lion's Cave in the mountain. Potatzio loves to shine, thats why its tail has some sparks that move when it moves.

Maparillo

This panda and raccoon hybrid, is the trickster of the island. Maparillo likes to play pranks to all the Bam Banimals, especially the island's leader right hand man. With Potatzio they do an acrobat show every full moon in the Bamboo Forest.

Kiwano

In the Goo Swamp on the east of the island, lives Kiwano. It is part chameleon and crocodrile, so it changes between green, pink and blue. Normally Kiwano likes to be alone with its toughts in the swamp, but when the Bam Banimals have a reunion, it is the life of the party!

Flabio

On the bottom of the ocean you can find a jellyfish and shark hybrid named Flabio. Like Kiwano, it is a solitary Bam Banimal and almost never leaves its home, but you can spot it easily thanks to its unique pink color.

Tucancamon

Tucancamon is the wisest Bam Banimal of all the land, thats why it is the right hand man of Akevia. Whenever something is wrong it is the first to think of ideas on how to solve it. Tucancamon is a toucan and a monkey hybrid, making it easy for it to get from one place to another. Eventhough some bam banimals play pranks to it, Tucancamon will always have their back, no matter what.

Akevia

The leader of the Bam Banimals, a mystical being named Akevia. It is a quetzal and axolotl hybrid with a very distinctive glow. You can find Akevia in the Lion's Cave near the north of the island. Akevia loves every single Bam Banimal, and worries for them all the time, but as the leader it needs to be strong and look out for all.
