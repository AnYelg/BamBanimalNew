Lore of the Story

Between levels, some part of the lore can appear in short clips that show how the player saved certain Bam Banimal, and why they were infected in the first place.

Animation and Scenes

Each Bam banimal was infected on a different part of the island, and the virus infected them in differnet ways, so to have a better storytelling, it will help the game to have different animations for each bam banimal and a new scenery for them.

Coding
